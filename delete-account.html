⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your game progress, scores, coins, purchased items, and personal data. This action cannot be undone.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Coming Soon)

We are working on adding an in-app account deletion feature. This will be available in a future update.

Option 2: Email Request (Available Now)

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

midnightadamstudios@gmail.com

Required Information in Your Email:

  1. Your in-game nickname or player name
  2. Your Google Play Games ID (if connected)
  3. Approximate date you started playing
  4. Confirmation that you want to delete ALL data

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Player profile and nickname
  • Game progress and scores
  • Coins and virtual currency
  • Purchased items and unlocks
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Advertising ID and analytics data
  • Crash logs associated with your device

What Data Will Be Retained

  • Google Play purchase history (required by Google for refunds)
  • Anonymous analytics (not linked to your identity)

Retention period: Purchase history is kept for 2 years per legal requirements.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.
